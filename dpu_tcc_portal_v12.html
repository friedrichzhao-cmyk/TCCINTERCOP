💡 Tips for 教学负荷上限  Maximum Teaching Load
1. 对于 8节课/周（如英语类课程），同一位老师在同一 Semester Block 时段内、同一年级前提下，能够担负的教学负荷上限为 2门课。
For 8-hr/week courses (e.g. English), within the same Semester Block and same cohort year, one faculty member's maximum teaching load is 2 courses.
2. 对于 12节课/周（如"计算机创意设计 Computer Creative Design"类课程），同一位老师在同一 Semester Block 时段内、不同年级前提下，能够担负的教学负荷上限也可以为 2门课。
For 12-hr/week courses (e.g. Computer Creative Design), within the same Semester Block across different cohort years, the maximum teaching load can also be 2 courses.
